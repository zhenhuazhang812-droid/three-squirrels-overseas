Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ varies by product, typically ranging from 200-1000 boxes. We offer flexible options for new partners - contact us to discuss your requirements.

What are your shipping options?

We ship via sea freight (FOB, CIF) for large orders and air freight for samples. We work with major shipping lines and can arrange door-to-door delivery to most destinations.

Can I customize packaging?

Yes! We offer full custom branding services including logo design, packaging design, and custom flavors. MOQ for custom packaging starts at 1,000 boxes.

What certifications do you have?

We are certified with ISO 22000, FSSC 22000, HACCP, BRC, and FDA. Our facilities are also HALAL and KOSHER certified for applicable products.
