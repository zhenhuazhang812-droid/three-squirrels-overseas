Our Story

From Anhui to the World: The Three Squirrels Story

Founded in 2012 in Hefei, Anhui Province, Three Squirrels started as a small online store with a big ambition: to bring premium Chinese snacks to every corner of the world.

Today, Three Squirrels is China's #1 nut and snack brand, with annual sales exceeding 5 billion RMB, serving distributors and retailers in over 20 countries across 5 continents.
