Our Story

Three Squirrels Overseas Center was founded in 2012 with a singular vision: to share the rich tapestry of Chinese snack culture with the world. What began as a small export operation has grown into a trusted global partner serving distributors, retailers, and foodservice companies across 50+ countries.

Our name draws inspiration from the industrious squirrel—symbolizing our commitment to carefully curating and delivering the finest snacks from China's diverse culinary landscape. Just as squirrels gather and preserve the best nature has to offer, we source, inspect, and deliver premium products that meet exacting international standards.

Today, we manage over 500 product SKUs and work with more than 200 global partners. Our success is built on three pillars: unwavering quality, transparent partnerships, and a genuine passion for sharing authentic Chinese flavors.

Our Mission

To be the bridge between Chinese snack heritage and global consumers, delivering authentic flavors that delight taste buds while upholding the highest standards of quality, safety, and reliability.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted Chinese snack export partner, recognized for our product excellence, customer service, and contribution to introducing Chinese culinary culture to every corner of the globe.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth from a local startup to a global export leader.

2012

Foundation

Three Squirrels Overseas Center established in Hefei, Anhui, with a small team of 10 passionate food enthusiasts.

2015

First International Shipment

Expanded to serve our first international clients in Southeast Asia, marking the beginning of our global journey.

2017

European Market Entry

Successfully entered the European market with full FDA and EU compliance certifications.

2019

Product Line Expansion

Grew our product portfolio to over 300 SKUs and established partnerships with 100+ global distributors.

2022

10th Anniversary

Celebrated serving 50+ countries with 500+ products and 200+ global partners across all continents.

2024

Innovation Center

Opened state-of-the-art product development center to create custom formulations for global partners.
