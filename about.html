About Three Squirrels

From a Single Apartment in Hefei to 20+ Countries Worldwide

Founded in 2012, Three Squirrels has grown into China's #1 nut and snack brand, listed on Shenzhen Stock Exchange (300783).

Our Journey

From one small idea to a global snack empire

2012

Founded in Hefei, Anhui

Zhang Yubo founded Three Squirrels in a small apartment with a bold vision: transform traditional Chinese snacks into a modern national brand.

2014

China's First 100M RMB Snack Brand on Tmall

Three Squirrels became the first nut brand on Alibaba's Tmall to exceed 100 million RMB in annual sales — setting a record for China's snack industry.

2016

Launched "Fulfill Dreams" Public Welfare Program

Committed to social responsibility, Three Squirrels launched its signature public welfare program, supporting education in rural China.

2019

Listed on Shenzhen Stock Exchange (300783)

Three Squirrels went public on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange, raising over 600 million RMB. The company reached a market cap of over 20 billion RMB at its peak.

2020

International Expansion — Three Squirrels Overseas

Launched dedicated overseas division to serve international distributors. Obtained FDA registration, HALAL certification, and established export channels to North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

Today

Serving 20+ Countries Worldwide

With 100+ product SKUs, 5 production bases, and a team of 3,000+ employees, Three Squirrels continues to grow as a global snack food brand.
