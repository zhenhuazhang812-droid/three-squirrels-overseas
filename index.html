About Three Squirrels

From a Small Apartment to a Global Brand

Founded in 2012 by Zhang Yubo in Hefei, Anhui Province, Three Squirrels began as a daring idea in a small apartment — to modernize China's traditional snack industry and bring it to the world.

By 2014, we became the first nut brand on Tmall to exceed 100 million RMB in annual sales. In 2019, we went public on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (stock code: 300783). Today, Three Squirrels operates 5 production bases, employs 3,000+ people, and serves distributors in over 20 countries.
